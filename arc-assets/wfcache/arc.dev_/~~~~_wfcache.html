Improving children’s and families’ lives one organization at a time

This website is for leaders and researchers committed to building child welfare and mental health providers that improve the lives of at-risk children and their families.

FOR LEADERS
Improving culture and climate belongs on every organization’s strategic plan.

FOR RESEARCHERS
We have the ONLY nationally-normed measure to help you . . .

Organizations with positive cultures and climates:

  • Have lower staff turnover
  • Do better with EBPs
  • Have better client outcomes

Our tools and strategies have been tested in “real world” organizations and proven across two decades of rigorous research funded by prestigious organizations such as the National Institute of Mental Health and the W.T. Grant Foundation. For a list of our publications click here.

Mission: We work collaboratively with leaders, researchers and others to increase the implementation capacity and overall effectiveness of child welfare and mental health organizations.

“Ironically, we are focused on the mental health and well-being of the people we serve and don’t see that our organizational processes are not so healthy. ARC helps leaders to be more aware and to use healthier processes.”

Gary Morse, Associate Executive Director, Places for People

“ARC leads to better outcomes, improved services, improved employee morale, and greater employee ownership of making changes within the agency.”

Katrina S. McDonald, Senior Vice President, Community-Based Behavioral Health, Crider Health Center
